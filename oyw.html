Virufy & One Young World

Our student-led iniative is a glove fit!

Why this will work

Collaboration

Virufy has been featured on various OYW platforms, including ambassador of the month in the Americas and Japan, ambassadors in action, and ambassadors on the frontline for COVID-19 response. We are also collaborating with OYW coordinating ambassadors across various regions for outreach efforts. Please stay tuned as we update this page!

Crowdsourcing Data

We need help from everyone around the world to make this solution work! To become a universal COVID detector, our solution depends on cough data from people of all countries and ethnicities. Please spread the word and donate your unique cough to us on our beta app!

Medical Outreach

To gather clinically valid cough data for research publication and government approval, we need to work with hospitals and clinics on the ground to have patients tested for COVID record their coughs with our app. Our clinical researchers and Stanford MDs have already prepared IRB forms and are on standby to conduct international research studies.

Press

Currently, we are coordinating mass outreach across Africa, Asia, and South America, where the virus is hitting hardest in order to gather the data we need to bring this solution to where it's needed most. Once the solution is ready, we plan to deploy it all over the world.
Please help us connect with the press and media outlets, so that we can spread the word about our efforts.

Donate

To support clinical data collection on the ground in the regions hardest hit by COVID-19, we need funding. As detailed on our crowdfunding campaign, funds will be directed to support medical costs such as protective equipment. We are also in need of grant funding to accelerate our efforts in lower income regions across Africa, Asia, and South America.

Volunteer

Our one young team is led by students dedicated to social good from reputable institutions like Stanford, Princeton, and One Young World. We have strong trust in members of the One Young World community and warmly welcome any ambassadors interested to pitch in. Please see our Join Us page.
